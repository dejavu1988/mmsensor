Development:
Assignment 1 for Mobile Middleware course.
This is an Android project developed in Eclipse (ADT), and tested with Motorola ME525 (Android 2.3.7).

Usage:
By starting the app and clicking on the button, it lists the sensors available on the device and shows their current readings numerically in a GUI.
